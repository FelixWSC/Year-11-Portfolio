Floor gradient

Development

The first function I made, its job is to create the floor gradient to create the illusion that the player is moving through the background and the pipes.

How this works is that a loop scans through the bottom row of the board, each time it draws two overlapping squares, one square is always black, and then a slightly smaller square is drawn on top of the black square, this gives the appearance of the slightly smaller square on top having an outline. The colour of the smaller square is determined by the columns and row_count which gives the square an RGB r value which means the squares will have a colour from one black to red.

Floor gradient

Development

The first function I made, its job is to create the floor gradient to create the illusion that the player is moving through the background and the pipes.

How this works is that a loop scans through every square on the bottom row of our board. Each time it draws two overlapping squares, with one slighlty smaller square on top of another one to give the appearance of a slight oultline for the pixels. One square is always black, which finds its correct position with the (col * square_size, row * square_Size) parameters. Then the height and width is just the square_size. The other square and the main floor is drawn by creating an RGB value based on the valuse of col and row_count (which is constant) this creates a gradient from black to red for our floor.
